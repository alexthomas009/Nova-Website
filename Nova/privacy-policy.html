Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

Welcome to Nova. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe environment for all our users, especially children and students. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection and usage, in strict compliance with the Google Play Families Policy, Apple's App Store Guidelines for Kids, the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR-K).

1. Data Collection & Age Boundaries

Nova is designed as a student productivity tool supporting users from age 10 to 30+. We have designed our app to prioritize data minimization and precise parental controls perfectly suited for each age bracket:

  • Ages 10-14 (Explorer Tier): Mandatory parental monitoring is built-in. Parents are kept fully informed of the child's usage inside the app. No location mapping or tracking of external behavior is done.
  • Ages 18 and older: Parental controls are entirely optional and fully student-controlled.
  • No Location Data: We do not track or collect precise location mapping.

2. Third-Party Services and SDKs

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising Software Development Kits (SDKs) that process user data or display interest-based advertising. Ensure peace of mind knowing that there are no third-party trackers monitoring your child's behavior.

3. Payments and Financial Data

For users purchasing premium features or subscriptions, we utilize the secure payment infrastructure provided by Apple and Google. Nova does not directly collect, process, or store your credit card or financial information. Any payment data is securely handled by the respective app stores under their privacy guidelines.

4. Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have full control over your child's data. Because all study data is stored locally on the device, you can instantly review or delete all information by simply unenstalling the app or clearing the app data in your device settings. If you have questions regarding data privacy, please contact us.

5. Advertising

Nova is entirely ad-free. We do not display any advertisements to children, ensuring an uninterrupted and completely safe digital environment.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated either via an update note in the App Store/Google Play Store or through a notice on this website.
